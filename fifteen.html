Battle of Plassey

(1757)

A decisive battle in the early phase of British rule in India, where the British East India Company defeated the Nawab of Bengal and established its control over Bengal.

Battle of Panipat

(1761)

One of the largest and bloodiest battles fought in Indian history, resulting in the victory of Ahmed Shah Durrani's Afghan forces over the Marathas and marking the beginning of their decline.

Battle of Plassey

(1799)

An epic battle fought between the British East India Company and the Kingdom of Mysore, led by Tipu Sultan. The British emerged victorious and ended Tipu Sultan's rule.

Battle of Buxar

(1764)

A significant battle that took place during the Bengal Presidency of British India. The East India Company's forces defeated the combined army of Mir Qasim, the Nawab of Bengal, and the Mughal Emperor Shah Alam II.

Battle of Haldighati

(1576)

A famous battle between the forces of Maharana Pratap, the Rajput ruler of Mewar, and the Mughal Empire led by Emperor Akbar. Although the Mughals claimed victory, Maharana Pratap's resistance became a symbol of Rajput valor.
