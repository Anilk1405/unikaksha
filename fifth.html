Q: What is the shipping cost?
A: Shipping is free for orders above 2000rs. For orders below that, the shipping cost is 199rs.
Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use that tracking number on our website to track your order.
Q: What is your return policy?
A: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in its original condition and packaging. Please contact our customer support to initiate a return.
Q: How long does it take to process an order?
A: Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Once processed, the delivery time depends on your location and the shipping method chosen.
Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. The shipping cost and delivery time may vary based on the destination.
